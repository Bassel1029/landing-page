Exploring the Wonders of the Cosmos

Milky Way: Our home galaxy, a spiral system with billions of stars and a supermassive black hole at its center. Spans 100,000 light-years

Horsehead Nebula: A dark, horse-shaped nebula in Orion, silhouetted against the glowing IC 434. A site of active star formation

Orion Nebula (M42): A bright stellar nursery, 1,344 light-years away, home to the Trapezium Cluster and part of the Orion Molecular Cloud Complex

Andromeda Galaxy (M31): The closest spiral galaxy to the Milky Way, 2.5 million light-years away, on course to merge with us in 4.5 billion years
